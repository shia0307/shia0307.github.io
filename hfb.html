Project Summary

Healthy Food Box is a local food delivery system that help promoting healthy eating habit at home and suporting local small farming business. This project aimed to build regional communities around preparing and eating healthy meals. The mobile application we proposed acted as a portal into a personalized produce delivery platform.

The food box is not the only thing we deliver to the users, through our application, users can access to all the recipes selected and shared by local farmers and community members.

How does it work

Triggered by the push notification, users received the latest recipes from our system. Once users submit their choice of recipes, local distributores will pack all the produce and ingredients and dispatch the food box as soon as possible. Food box will be delivered to the designated location within the timeframe users choose.
