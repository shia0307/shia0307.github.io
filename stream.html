Stream is a system which captures happy moments automatically so you can put the camera away and enjoy the happiest moments of your life.

Duration

10 Weeks with Microsoft Instructor

Team Member

Jeremy Friedland, Mallika Simone, Willie Franklin, Ariana Zukowski, Enrique Dominguez

Applied Skills

Video Production, UI Design

Deliverables

Video Prototype, Modile Prototype, Final Presentation, Process Book

Project Summary

Triggered by biometric data, audio and location data collected through our small wearable device, Stream captures the true and natural happy moments of your life. This allows you to be centered in your happy moment without having to manually capture it. You can then review these captured streams in a variety of ways on a tablet.



This project was created in response to the theme "Billions of Sensors" for the 2014 Microsoft Design Expo:

"What would you do with a billion sensors?"
