About Beermiscuous

If a café and a brewery had a love child, it would probably look a lot like Beermiscuous. The Lakeview joint serves beers from more than 30 Chicagoland breweries in a setting that feels more like a cozy coffee shop than a bar. Beeristas certified by the Cicerone Certification Program present pours in the warmly lit space lined with natural wood, taking care to maintain ideal serving temperatures and use proper glassware. Free WiFi and a friendly outside-food policy are the icing on the beer-soaked cake, making Beermiscuous a favorite for first dates and solo excursions alike.
